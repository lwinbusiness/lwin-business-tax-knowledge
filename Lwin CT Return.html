English

  • Do not include shareholder loans, capital injections, refundable deposits, VAT collected, or transfers between the company’s own bank accounts.
  • If invoices are unpaid but recognised under accrual accounting, they may still be included.
  • Foreign income may later be exempt or relieved, but the gross revenue base must first be considered.

Cash basis is generally available where revenue does not exceed AED 3M. Above that, do not select cash basis in an ordinary case. Accrual basis is the normal accounting basis and has no upper revenue cap.

English

Advanced consultant explanation — Realisation Basis

မြန်မာ

Realisation basis ဆိုတာ cash basis မဟုတ်ပါ။ Accrual financial statements ထဲမှာ fair value / impairment / unrealised gain or loss တွေပါလာနိုင်တဲ့အခါ၊ အချို့ gain/loss တွေကို tax အတွက် actual sale, disposal, settlement, transfer, write-off စတဲ့ realisation event ဖြစ်မှ tax effect ထည့်မယ်ဆိုတဲ့ election ဖြစ်ပါတယ်။

  • No — နောက်ထပ်မေးခွန်းမလာ။ Simple trading/service company၊ fair value asset မရှိတဲ့ company တွေမှာ ပုံမှန်အားဖြင့် No လုံလောက်နိုင်ပါတယ်။
  • Yes — Bank/Insurance question ပေါ်မယ်။ Accrual basis FS ရှိပြီး unrealised gain/loss exposure ရှိတဲ့ company တွေမှာသာ စဉ်းစားပါ။
  • Bank/Insurance = Yes — scope choice ကို portal က auto/disabled လုပ်နိုင်ပါတယ်။ Bank/Insurance providers အတွက် law က narrower election route ကို သတ်မှတ်ထားလို့ဖြစ်ပါတယ်။
  • Bank/Insurance = No — scope ကို taxpayer ကရွေးရမယ်။ Scope A က fair value/impairment items ကို focus; Scope B က capital-account assets/liabilities ကို broader way နဲ့သက်ရောက်နိုင်ပါတယ်။

English

Realisation basis is not cash basis. It is an election for a taxpayer preparing financial statements on the accrual basis to recognise certain gains and losses for Corporate Tax only when a realisation event occurs, rather than taxing or deducting every unrealised accounting movement immediately.

  • No — no further question appears. This may be appropriate for simple trading/service businesses with no material unrealised fair-value exposure.
  • Yes — the Bank/Insurance question appears. Consider this only where accrual accounts contain material unrealised gains/losses.
  • Bank/Insurance = Yes — the scope question is auto-disabled because banks and insurance providers are restricted to the relevant statutory route.
  • Bank/Insurance = No — the taxpayer must select the scope of the realisation-basis election.

When YES may be justified

Investment property fair value gains, securities measured at fair value, FX/fair-value liability movements, or major unrealised gains/losses in accrual accounts.

When NO may be safer

No material fair-value assets/liabilities, simple inventory/service business, weak accounting support, or uncertainty about long-term consequences.

Failure condition

Your judgment fails if the accounts contain material unrealised fair-value gains/losses but the election impact was not modelled before filing.

  • Accounting Income သည် CT computation ရဲ့ starting point ဖြစ်ပြီး Tab 3 Net profit/(loss) နဲ့ချိတ်ထားပါတယ်။ ဒီနေရာမှာ number ကို manual မပြင်သင့်ပါ။ အမှားရှိရင် Tab 3 / trial balance mapping ကိုပြင်ရပါမယ်။
  • Equity Method ဆိုတာ associate / joint venture investment တွေကို cost သို့မဟုတ် fair value မဟုတ်ဘဲ investor ရဲ့ share of profit/loss ဖြင့် accounts ထဲမှာ record လုပ်တဲ့ method ပါ။ Yes ဆိုရင် share of profit/loss fields ပေါ်ပြီး CT computation မှာ double counting / exempt income / participation exemption analysis လိုနိုင်ပါတယ်။
  • Income statement ထဲမဝင်တဲ့ gain/loss ဆိုတာ OCI/equity/revaluation reserve စတဲ့နေရာမှာ financial statements ထဲအသိအမှတ်ပြုပေမယ့် နောက်ပိုင်း P&L ထဲကိုမပြန်ဝင်မယ့် realised/unrealised gains/losses ဖြစ်နိုင်ပါတယ်။ Yes ဆိုရင် schedule ဖြည့်ရပါတယ်။
  • No ရွေးရမယ့် case က equity method investments မရှိခြင်း၊ OCI/equity ထဲမှာ non-recycling gain/loss မရှိခြင်း၊ accounts ထဲက income/loss အားလုံး P&L မှာပဲပါခြင်းတို့ဖြစ်ပါတယ်။

English

  • Accounting Income is the starting accounting profit/loss for CT computation and is linked to Tab 3 net profit/loss. Do not manually override it here; correct the accounting schedule or trial balance mapping.
  • Equity Method normally applies to investments in associates or joint ventures where the investor records its share of investee profit or loss. If Yes, the return requires the share of profits/losses to be separately identified for CT adjustment and exemption analysis.
  • Gains/losses outside the income statement can include items recognised in OCI/equity/revaluation reserves that will not subsequently recycle to profit or loss. If Yes, the schedule is required.
  • No is appropriate where there are no equity-accounted investments and no realised/unrealised gains or losses recognised in financial statements outside the income statement that will not later be recognised in P&L.

Example 1 – Equity method Yes

Company owns 30% of an associate and records AED 120,000 share of associate profit in its accounts. Equity Method = Yes; share of profits field should be completed.

Example 2 – Outside P&L Yes

Financial asset fair value gain is recorded in OCI and will not recycle to P&L. Answer Yes and complete the outside-income-statement schedule.

Example 3 – Ordinary company No

No associates/JVs and no OCI/equity gain/loss. Both questions can normally be answered No.

Common mistake: Do not answer Yes merely because the company has investments, bank balances, receivables, or foreign exchange differences. The test is the accounting treatment in the financial statements, not the existence of an asset.

Non-deductible expenditure – consultant explanation / မြန်မာ + English

မြန်မာ

  • Entertainment – client entertainment, meals, events စတဲ့ expenses တွေက CT law အရ full deductible မဟုတ်နိုင်ပါ။ Accounting P&L ထဲမှာ expense ဖြစ်ပေမယ့် tax computation ထဲမှာ non-deductible portion ကို add-back လုပ်ရနိုင်ပါတယ်။
  • Pension contributions – UAE law/approved scheme အောက်မဟုတ်တဲ့ pension contribution မျိုးတွေကို စစ်ရပါတယ်။
  • Non-QPBE donations – Qualifying Public Benefit Entity မဟုတ်တဲ့ party ကိုပေးတဲ့ donation သည် generally deductible support မရှိနိုင်ပါ။
  • Exempt income related expenses – exempt income ရဖို့တိုက်ရိုက်ကုန်ကျတဲ့ expense ကို ordinary deductible expense လို့မထားသင့်ပါ။
  • Owner distributions – dividend/profit distribution ကို expense လို့သုံးထားရင် tax add-back လုပ်ရမယ့် high-risk item ဖြစ်ပါတယ်။

English

  • Entertainment expenditure may be partly or wholly non-deductible depending on the CT rules and nature of the cost.
  • Pension contributions require support that they are permitted under the relevant UAE rules or qualifying scheme.
  • Donations to entities that are not Qualifying Public Benefit Entities should not be treated as automatically deductible.
  • Expenditure linked to exempt income should be identified separately because exempt income should not be paired with ordinary deductible expenses.
  • Owner distributions are not business expenses. If booked in P&L, they normally require tax add-back.
Common mistake: bookkeeping category alone is not decisive. A cost being recorded in QuickBooks/Xero as expense does not make it deductible for UAE Corporate Tax.

  • The brought-forward question asks whether the taxpayer wants to deduct brought-forward net interest expenditure in the current Tax Period. It does not create an extra question on this screen.
  • Judgment failure condition: answering Yes merely because the company has a bank loan. The legal trigger is the AED 12 million net interest expenditure threshold, considering carried-forward amounts.

မြန်မာ

  • Related Party transaction = Yes ဆိုတာ shareholder, director/manager, group company, common-control company, related individual/company တို့နဲ့ sale, service, loan, royalty, asset transfer, management fee စတဲ့ transaction ရှိတာပါ။

English

  • Answer Yes where the taxpayer transacted with shareholders, directors/managers, group companies, entities under common control, or other related parties.
